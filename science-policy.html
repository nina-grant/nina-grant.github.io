Science Policy & Civic Leadership

From monitoring policy developments to directly engaging decision-makers at local, national, and international levels

My goal is to help bridge those worlds, whether that means answering questions about solar radiation modification, explaining agricultural risks, or simply listening and translating concerns back into research questions.
